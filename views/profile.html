Rules

  • The number of rounds in the game is unlimited.
  • The right of the first move is determined randomly.
  • Victory is considered a complete loss of opponent's hp.
  • You only have a minute to do a your turn. After the minute is up, you will lose and your opponent will win.
  • Each player has 3 cards. When you use the attack or ability of a certain card 3 or more times - it will be replaced by another random card in next round.
  • There are 8 units of energy per round:

    • - Each ability uses 2 energy,
    - Hit 3 energy,
    - Switch 1 energy.

  • The player has the opportunity to press the "Next Round" button if he wants to end his moves in the current one (for example, due to running out of energy).
  • One card can use only one ability per round.
  • Abilities do not stack. If several abilities are activated on one character, the buff is taken from the first one.
  • Character switching and hitting count as a turn, using an ability does not.
  • If a player leaves the game, the victory is awarded to his opponent.
